The purpose of the app is to demo the ts.validator.fluent TypeScript based generic validation framework.

  • There is a validation service in the app.
  • This service uses the ts.validator framework for model validations.
  • This service is injected into the Login and Register components.
  • The validation result is displayed in tooltips.
Advantages of a validation service approach:

  • The business rules around validation are separated from the components.
  • All business rules are centralized in one place.
  • The service is re-usable.
  • The service can be injected into any component.
  • You can unit test the business rules by unit testing the service.
